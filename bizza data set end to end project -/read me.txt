
📊 Pizza Sales Dashboard (Power BI Project)
🧩 Project Overview
This project analyzes pizza sales performance using real transactional data.


The goal is to identify sales trends, top-performing products, and customer behavior to support better business decisions for a pizza restaurant.

The dashboard provides a clear overview of revenue, orders, and sales patterns across different time periods and product Type & size..



🎯 Objectives

- Understand total sales performance by day, time, and pizza Type & size.

- Identify best-selling pizzas and those with low demand.

- Analyze sales by time of day (morning, noon, evening, night) to optimize staffing and promotions.

- Provide actionable insights to improve inventory planning and marketing strategies.

🛠️ Tools & Techniques

- Power BI for data visualization and dashboard creation

- Excel / CSV dataset as data source

- Data Cleaning & Transformation using Power Query

- DAX measures for calculating total revenue, average order value, and sales by Type & size.


📈 Key Insights

- Evening & Midday hours show the highest order volume and revenue.

- Classic and Supreme pizzas are the top-selling categories.

- Despite fewer orders, Large-sized pizzas contribute the most to total revenue.

- Extend evening & Midday staff shifts and introduce bundle offers.
- Customer Behavior at Weekends show 17% higher average order sold &value.

🚀 Future Enhancements

- Add customer segmentation to understand repeat buyers.

- Integrate monthly trend forecasting using Power BI predictive visuals.
